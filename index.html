Growth is a AURA









Daily Motivational Reminders:
These are short quotes or reminders that get delivered throughout the day to your students to inspire positivity, help them feel empowered, and keep them focused on their goals. They help in so-called mental boosts in times of stress, self-doubt.


Daily Study Tracker:
This feature enables students to record and track their study durations, which enhances their consistency, patterns, and time management. It cultivates a mindset of growth and responsibility in their educational process.


To-Do List:
A systematic and dynamic to-do list can aid students in listing out tasks, prioritizing tasks and marking them as complete. It avoids overstimulation and helps in forming a clear goal connecting mindset.


Mindfulness Prompts:
Gentle reminders for students to take a moment to breathe deeply and check in with their feelings. They help keep emotional regulation, ease anxiety, and promote overall mental wellness.
